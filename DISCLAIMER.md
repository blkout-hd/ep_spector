### Purpose Statement
This project is a **technical research framework** for document analysis, knowledge graph construction, and information retrieval. It is designed for:
- Academic research into document processing techniques
- Analysis of publicly available government documents
- Development of open-source intelligence tools
- Educational purposes in data science and NLP

### Data Sources
**ALL DATA MUST BE FROM PUBLICLY AVAILABLE SOURCES ONLY**

This system is designed to process:
- FOIA-released documents (public domain)
- Government publications available on official websites
- Court records from PACER (public access)
- News articles and public records
- Open-source intelligence (OSINT) from verified sources

- Unauthorized access to protected computer systems

- Any activity violating CFAA, GDPR, or applicable laws

The PDF redaction analysis features are provided for:
- **Academic research** into document security

- **Security auditing** of one's own documents

**DO NOT** use these techniques to:
- Access classified information
